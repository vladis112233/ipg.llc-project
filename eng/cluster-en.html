Cluster development

Our consulting company specializes in supporting and developing agroclusters, acting as a cluster development agent. We offer comprehensive services to strengthen the competitiveness of agroclusters, promoting their sustainable growth and prosperity.

Our services include:

Strategic planning for agroclusters:

Development of long-term strategies for the effective development of agroclusters, taking into account market peculiarities and regional factors.

Analysis of opportunities for diversification and expansion of agroclusters to ensure sustainable growth and resilient competitiveness.

Competitiveness analysis of agroclusters:

Evaluation of the strengths and weaknesses of agroclusters to develop strategies for enhancing competitiveness and achieving optimal results.

Comparative analysis with other agroclusters to identify key aspects that will enable competitive advantages.

Development of agroclusters:

Development of specific action plans to create a favorable environment that promotes the development of agroclusters and supports their sustainable growth.

Providing recommendations for marketing and promotion strategies aimed at attracting new investments and supporting the sustainable development of agroclusters.

Support for the development of the cluster's institutional network:

Providing incentives for public and private organizations aimed at supporting the development of agroclusters and increasing their capacity.

Encouraging local, regional, and national institutions, including chambers of commerce, local authorities, NGOs, producer associations, universities, educational institutions, and regional economic development agencies, to actively support the development of agroclusters.

Identification and attraction of talent:

Identifying the needs of agroclusters for talents and companies and developing specific measures to attract them.

Building an investment and talent attraction policy based on strengthening the competitive advantages of the agrocluster.

Public policies on economic development and competitiveness:

Providing tools and recommendations to local, regional, and national governments to determine economic development programs and competitiveness policies in agroclusters.
