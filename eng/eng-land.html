About US

IPG (Innovation. Partnership. Generation.) is an international company in advanced technologies that covers all stages of value creation from initial production to final consumption. Our innovative solutions and strategies span primary production in agribusiness, processing stages, storage, through the creation of innovative logistic hubs and corresponding infrastructure. Our goal is to provide strategic support to our partners and contribute to sustainable development in the face of constant changes.
